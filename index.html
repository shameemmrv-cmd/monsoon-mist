Some homes are built for the sun.
This one was built for the rain.

Chapter One · The Atmosphere
01 · Dawn

Cradled by mist
and minarets.

02 · The Valley

Where the valley
wakes in gold.

03 · A Glimpse

A glance through leaves —
a glow in the distance.

The home reveals itself slowly.
First as light. Then as silence.

Chapter Two · The Approach
04 · Sanctuary

Built in the
shadow of grace.

05 · After the Rain

The earth listens.
The stones remember.

06 · The Clearing

A clearing
the forest agreed to keep.

Chapter Three · The Reveal

And then — the home,
standing still in the rain.

07 · The Home

Warm light.
Wild rain.
One home.

08 · Twilight

Lit from within,
framed by the wild.

09 · The Doorway

An invitation,
warmly worded.

10 · The Hush

When the forest
forgets the world.

Look closer. The home speaks in details —
in beams, in beams of light, in the patience of wood.

Chapter Four · The Details
11 · The Roofline

Where craft
meets the sky.

12 · A Pause

One small flame,
holding the night.

13 · The Geometry

Lines drawn
by patient hands.

14 · The Window

A window
that listens
to the rain.

15 · The Threshold

Cross this
threshold slowly.

16 · From Above

Held softly
by green walls.

17 · The Wait

Evening arrives,
slowly.
The home waits.

Chapter Five · The Soul

A home built in the company
of something sacred.

Just beyond the trees, a mosque stands — pale, patient, eternal.
This home was placed here on purpose. Close enough to hear the call,
far enough to keep the silence. A neighbour to grace.

18 · The Soul

In the company
of something sacred.

Other Possibilities

Explored in other lights.

The same home, dressed in two more moods.

Option I In sun and stone
Option II In wood and warmth

A home is not its walls.
A home is the light it keeps,
the rain it remembers.
